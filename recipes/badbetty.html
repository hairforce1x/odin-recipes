Bad Betty

Description

Grapfruit vodka, a splash of gin, and blood orange sweet and sour plus ginger-ale make this a juicy and refreshing cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Deep Eddy Grapfruit Vodka
  • .5 oz gin
  • 2 oz Finest Call Blood Orange Sour
  • 1oz of ginger-ale

Steps

  1. Fill shaker with ice.
  2. Add ingredients (except for ginger-ale) to shaker.
  3. Cover shaker and shake vigorously.
  4. Pour into pint glass.
  5. Top with ginger-ale
  6. Pour, garnish with grapefruit slice in the drink, and enjoy.
