Badass Margarita

Description

So powerful you can only have two! The secret ingredient is apple juice concentrate.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz tequila
  • 1.5 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz apple juice concentrate
  • .5 oz simple syrup

Steps

  1. Fill shaker with ice.
  2. Add ingredients to shaker.
  3. Cover shaker and shake vigorously.
  4. Rim a pint glass with salt if desired.
  5. Pour, garnish with a lime wedge on the rim, and enjoy!
