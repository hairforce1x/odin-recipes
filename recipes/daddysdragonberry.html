Daddy's Dragonberry

Description

Bacardi Dragonberry Rum muddled with fresh strawberries and blueberries then shaken with house-made sweet and sour. Super fresh with the right balance of sweet and tart.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bacardi Dragonberry Rum
  • 1 strawberry, sliced
  • 8 blueberries
  • 4 oz sweet and sour
  • Splash of lemon-lime soda

Steps

  1. Muddle strawberry and blueberries in shaker.
  2. Fill shaker with ice.
  3. Add ingredients (except for lemon-lime soda) to shaker.
  4. Cover shaker and shake vigorously.
  5. Pour into pint glass.
  6. Top with lemon-lime soda
  7. Pour, garnish with two lemon slices on the rim (it's not a Dragonberry until it gets it's wings), and enjoy.
